Dilbert's "Salary Theorem" states that "Engineers and scientists can never earn as much as administrators and sales people." This theorem can now be proved mathematically:
  1. Given: Power = Work / Time and, Knowledge is Power
  2. Substituting Knowledge for Power, we obtain: Knowledge = Work / Time
  3. If Time = Money, then: Knowledge = Work / Money
  4. Solving this equation for Money, we obtain: Money = Work / Knowledge
Therefore, as Knowledge approaches zero, Money approaches infinity, regardless of the amount of Work done.
